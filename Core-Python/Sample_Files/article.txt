We use This for something near and singular. 
We use That for something far and singular. 
We use These for something near and plural. 
We use Those for something far and plural. 
These are the basics.

We use the words this, that, these, and those when we want to show things to other people. 
The main difference is if the things are singular or plural and also if they are near or far. 
This is English Grammar and these are commonly used words.
